Schedule¶

Workshop leaders will assist participants in transforming an existing course into a modular course using cloud computing resources available at Indiana University. Participants will create modules including video, lessons and tutorials.

Day Three (Sunday June 12)¶

12:00 - 1:30 Working Lunch and Wrap-up
